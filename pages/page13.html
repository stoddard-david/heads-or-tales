The laboratory was very simple. The walls and floor were a plain and polished grey. A lone table stood in the middle of room with a few strange tools upon it. The few selves along the wall were mostly empty, but one item caught your eye and Alfred’s attention.

Alfred immediately begin scanning the small egg. Its gears whirred away in the small cage. Alfred scanned the object over and over as numbers and data flashed across your screen. Alfred seems confused and frustrated by the device. You move closer to examine the small egg. Small purple and gold gems blink on and off inside.

“What is the matter Alfred?”

After a pause Alfred, “Dave, the device is completely contained, yet it isn't losing any momentum. It has been running for thousands of years without an outside power source.”

You eyes widen even more, “A perpetual motion machine, but that is impossible!”

“Yes it is Dave, but here it is.”

You cradle the device in one hand. “Alfred, this will change everything. Prep the ship Alfred. We are going home.”
