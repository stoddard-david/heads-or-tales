“Dave, we are approaching Janus-42. We will be there within an hour.”

You sigh a breath of relief. As an explore you spend a lot of time in space, and after over 3 months you have almost reached your destination. You can rest your feet on solid rock again in just a few hours, if the preliminary readings are correct.

“Thank you, Alfred,” you say to your small droid as it docks onto your cybernetic arm with a click.

The droid’s real name is T.I.G.-1999, but you always called the droid . It felt more personal and provided more life to your little friend.
